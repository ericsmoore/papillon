Could it all just be a dream? 


The idea gives you no comfort, but you close your 
eyes. Is is not a beautiful day to lay for hours in 
a bed of grass? 


Then doze there, warmed by the rising sun, cooled 
by the stubborn ground. You cannot be lost, 
confused, or alone if you are busy with summer 
dreams. Sleep a while. Worry not, the day is young.
